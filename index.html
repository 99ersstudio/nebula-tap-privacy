Privacy Policy — Nebula Tap: Space Idle Clicker

Effective date: 2026-03-16

This policy describes what data Nebula Tap collects, how it is used, and your choices.

1. Who We Are

Nebula Tap: Space Idle Clicker is developed by 99ers Studio.

2. Data We Collect

a) Game Progress (local only)

Your upgrade levels, credit totals, settings, and sound preferences are saved to private storage on your device. This data never leaves your device.

b) Anonymous User ID

When the online leaderboard is active, Firebase Authentication creates an anonymous identifier (UID) so the app can store your score. This UID cannot be linked back to your real identity.

c) Leaderboard Data

Your display name and score are stored in Google Cloud Firestore and are visible to other players on the leaderboard.

d) Crash Reports

Firebase Crashlytics collects crash logs, device model, and OS version to help us identify and fix bugs. No personal information is included in crash reports.

e) Advertising ID

Google AdMob collects your device advertising ID (AD_ID) to serve and measure ads. AdMob may also collect device and network information as described in Google's privacy policy.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Game progress: to save and restore your game state
  • Anonymous UID and leaderboard score: to rank players
  • Crash logs: to diagnose and fix app crashes
  • Advertising ID: to serve relevant ads

4. Data Sharing

  • Leaderboard scores and display names are visible to other players.
  • Advertising ID is shared with Google AdMob for ad serving.
  • Crash data is processed by Google Firebase Crashlytics.
  • We do not sell any data to third parties.

5. Third-Party Services

This app uses the following Google services:

  • Firebase Authentication (anonymous sign-in)
  • Cloud Firestore (leaderboard storage)
  • Firebase Crashlytics (crash reporting)
  • Google AdMob (advertising)
  • User Messaging Platform (GDPR/privacy consent)

These services are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Data Security

All data transmitted between the app and cloud services is encrypted using TLS (Transport Layer Security).

7. Data Retention

  • Game progress: stored locally until you uninstall or reset
  • Leaderboard data: retained in Firestore until deleted
  • Crash logs: retained for 90 days (Firebase default)
  • Advertising data: per Google Ads data retention policy

8. Children

This app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The anonymous UID used for the leaderboard cannot identify any individual.

9. Your Choices

  • Reset game progress via Settings → Reset Progress
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data
  • Request server-side data deletion by contacting us
  • Manage ad preferences via your device's ad settings
  • Manage consent preferences via the in-app consent dialog

10. Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will revise the effective date above and note the change in the app store listing.

11. Contact

Questions or data deletion requests? Reach us at: 99ers.dev@gmail.com
